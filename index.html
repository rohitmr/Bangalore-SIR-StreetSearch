Jayamahal 2002 Electoral Roll Finder

Find the correct 2002 electoral-roll part number for your road in the former Jayamahal Assembly Constituency (A087), Bengaluru — then jump straight to the official PDF.

This is an independent, community-built lookup tool. It is not run by the Election Commission or CEO Karnataka. All electoral roll PDFs are linked directly from ceo.karnataka.gov.in. Road-to-part mappings were reconstructed by OCR and manual verification and may contain errors — always confirm against the linked PDF page before relying on it.

Tip: try current names, old/historical names, or just part of a name (e.g. "Vyalikaval", "4th Cross", "RBI Colony"). Spelling doesn't need to be exact.

Pins show approximate locations of roads/localities in the 2002 index (geocoded automatically — treat placement as indicative, not exact). Click a pin for its part number and PDF link.
